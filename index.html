Wilderness Conservation

I take pride in my contribution to The Wilderness Society's efforts on the CORE Act, where I created a special feature within Trekking Maps to showcase the proposed protected areas. I also presented the app at various events to raise awareness for the CORE Act. Our unified efforts have led to the designation of the Camp Hale-Continental Divide National Monument and a 20-year mineral withdrawal on the Thompson Divide. Join me in supporting The Wilderness Society to ensure future generations are able to experience our wild places.
