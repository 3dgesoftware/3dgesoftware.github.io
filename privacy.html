Privacy Policy

Last Updated

August 14, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy is for the Trekking Maps app developed by 3DGE Software, LLC. It outlines how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the app. The app accesses our servers to retrieve map data, including imagery, terrain, and vector information, which is too large to be included in the app itself. The app may request your GPS location to display your position on the map, but this information is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information about you directly. However, we may collect certain technical information when you use the Trekking Maps app. This information includes:

  • IP address: We generally do not log IP addresses. However, in rare cases, such as investigating a security incident or technical issue, we may temporarily enable IP address logging. This information would be used solely for troubleshooting purposes and then deleted.
  • Map tile requests: Information about which map tiles you download may be logged for performance analysis and optimization. These requests are not associated with your IP address.
  • Search queries: When you use the app's search function, your search query is sent to a third-party service called LocationIQ to provide search results. No personal information is shared with LocationIQ. LocationIQ may collect technical information, such as your IP address and search queries, for analytics and service improvement purposes.

How We Use This Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Improving the Trekking Maps app: We analyze map tile request data to optimize app performance and identify areas for improvement.
  • Troubleshooting and security: In the unlikely event of technical issues or security concerns, we may use server logs (which may contain IP addresses) for troubleshooting purposes. However, as stated previously, this information is not retained.

By understanding how users interact with the app, we can enhance the overall user experience.

What Information We Share

We do not share any personal information with third parties. Any data collected, as described above, is used solely for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy and is not shared with external organizations for marketing or other purposes.

When you perform a location-based search, the search query is sent to a third-party service called LocationIQ to provide search results. No personal information is shared with LocationIQ.

Permissions

To provide the best possible user experience, Trekking Maps may request certain permissions. These permissions are optional and can be managed through your device's settings:

  • Location: You may be asked to grant permission to access your device's location. This is used to display your position on the map and for features such as recording GPX tracks.

Data Security

Data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted to protect your information.

Data Retention

While we do not actively retain any personal information, server logs may contain technical data, such as map tile requests, for a limited period for troubleshooting and performance analysis purposes.
